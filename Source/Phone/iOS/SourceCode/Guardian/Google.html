Non-Registered User:
  1. Setup buddies and preferences through phone
  2. Self-Tracking and showing route
  3. Ability to send SOS signals
    • Pre-defined SMS with location to buddies
    • Posting to FB (to a private group)
  4. One click call/SMS/Email to buddies while in SOS
  5. Local help (Police, Hospitals)
  6. Route to Local Help
  7. Local Emergency Numbers
 Registered User:
  1. All features of Non-Registered Users
  2. Allowing buddies to acknowledge (taking care of privacy)
  3. Adding Security groups to monitor you in real-time, when needed
  4. Ability to send SOS signals
    • Initiating immediate call with a buddy
    • Sending automated emails with your location to your buddies
    • SMS with Tracking trail to buddies
    • Sending pictures to the buddies
    • Sending pictures to your Security Groups
  5. Continuous SOS notifications via SMS, Email, FB Posts to buddies at regular interval, at absolutely NO COST
  6. Send SOS in the event of the phone being destroyed/ switched off
  7. Start SOS notifications by sending SMS when GPRS(internet) is unavailable
  8. Safety message after turning Off SOS
  9. Tracking people who have added you as Buddies, on your mobile devices
  10. Being tracked by your buddies in real-time on their mobile devices
  11. Being tracked by more than one organization's security group in real-time when you need
  12. Ability to save SOS photos in cloud for evidence
  13. Incident reporting like Eve-tease, accidents etc. along with evidence in cloud
  14. Security groups can help you by having ability to send Police/Medical Help to you when in SOS
  15. Allowing security groups to assign their marshals to rescue you on the fly with real-time tracking
  16. One click to start SOS - Pin SOS tile to Start page
  17. One click to restore all your buddies, preferences and settings on a new device
  18. One Drive access requested during registration is to archive your tracking information while we use data periodically. This feature will be part of future release. We DO NOT access any of your personal or public data/ information from onedrive now nor would do in future.
